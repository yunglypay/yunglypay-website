1. About Yungly Pay

Yungly Pay is an early-stage fintech concept founded by Adhil Rahman A, based in Kerala, India. The product is currently in prototype and partnership exploration stage, with a focus on building a teen-first prepaid UPI wallet through licensed banking/PPI partners.

When launched through a licensed partner, Yungly Pay is designed to provide teenagers with a mobile wallet, the ability to send and receive money, and access to partner discounts — all within limits set by RBI guidelines and guardian controls.

Any regulated payment, wallet, or banking services will be offered only through licensed banking/PPI partners, subject to applicable regulatory approvals and partner onboarding. Yungly Pay itself does not operate as a bank, NBFC, or PPI issuer.

4. Wallet & transactions

  • Your Yungly Pay wallet is a prepaid instrument — you can only spend money that has been loaded into it
  • Wallet loading limits will be set in accordance with RBI PPI guidelines and may vary based on your KYC level
  • Transactions are processed in real time and are generally irreversible once confirmed
  • If a transaction fails and money is deducted, it will be refunded within 3–5 business days, subject to partner bank timelines
  • We are not responsible for losses arising from transactions you authorise, even if you were deceived into doing so
  • Spending limits may be set by your guardian and are enforced by the app

Yungly Pay is designed to reduce failed transactions through reliable payment infrastructure and partner-backed rails. However, we cannot guarantee uninterrupted transaction success as this is subject to partner bank/PPI infrastructure and network conditions.

5. Fees

Yungly Pay is designed with minimal fees. Planned fee structure (subject to partner and regulatory approvals):

  • Account creation: Free
  • Wallet loading: Free (subject to banking partner charges)
  • Peer-to-peer transfers: Free up to limits set by RBI
  • Merchant payments: Free for the user; merchant interchange fee may apply
  • Wallet withdrawal to bank: As per RBI guidelines

We will always notify you of any fee before you confirm a transaction. We will give at least 30 days notice before introducing any new fees. Final fee structure will be confirmed upon partner and regulatory onboarding.
