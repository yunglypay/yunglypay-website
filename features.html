Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for how Indian teens use money — every feature has a reason to exist.

Core
⚡

Reliable transactions

Our payment infrastructure is built for reliability. Built to reduce failed transactions through reliable payment infrastructure and partner-backed rails.

Savings
🎯

Savings goals

Set a goal — new phone, sneakers, concert tickets — and watch your progress. Money saved automatically each week.

Control
🎛️

Flexible spending limits

Limits that actually make sense for a teen. Adjust weekly or monthly — no rigid bank-style caps.

Card
🃏

Physical + virtual card

A sleek physical card delivered to your door, plus an instant virtual card for online spending from day one.

Insights
📊

Spending insights

See exactly where your money goes — food, gaming, shopping — with beautiful visual breakdowns every week.

Speed
💸

Instant pocket money

Parents send money in seconds. No waiting, no delays. It hits your account before you can even ask twice.

Safety
🔒

Instant card freeze

Lost your card? One tap to freeze it instantly from the app. Unfreeze anytime when you find it.

UPI
📱

All UPI apps supported

Designed to work with India's UPI ecosystem, subject to partner and regulatory approvals — whatever UPI app you already use, Yungly fits right in.

Split with friends

Split bills with friends instantly. No more awkward "pay me back later" conversations.

Pocket money, reinvented

Get paid instantly.
Every time.

No more waiting for your parents to find cash, go to an ATM, or remember what day it is. With Yungly, pocket money hits your account the moment your parent sends it.

  • Instant bank transfer
  • Parent notification when you spend
  • Set recurring pocket money schedules
  • Request top-ups with one tap

actually matters.

Create savings goals for anything — the new iPhone, Air Jordans, a trip with friends. Set a target, pick a date, and Yungly helps you get there automatically.

  • Auto-save a % of every top-up

  • Celebrate when you hit your goal 🎉
