For Parents

You're in control.
They feel free.

Yungly gives your teen financial independence while keeping you informed and in control — without the arguments.

👁️

Real-time visibility

See every transaction the moment it happens. Know exactly where your teen's money is going — food, gaming, shopping, transport.

💸

Send money instantly

Transfer pocket money in seconds from any bank account. Set it once as a weekly or monthly recurring transfer and never think about it again.

🎛️

Set spending limits

Define daily or monthly spending limits. Your teen can spend freely within those limits — and you both sleep well.

🔔

Instant alerts

Get notified instantly for every transaction. Unusual spending? You'll know right away — not at the end of the month.

📵

Freeze the card anytime

If something doesn't look right, freeze your teen's card instantly from your app. One tap, immediate effect.

📈

Teach financial habits

Yungly shows your teen weekly spending summaries and savings progress. Real financial literacy, learned by doing.

How it works

Set up in minutes.

Getting your teen on Yungly is faster than an ATM trip.

1

Download the app

Both you and your teen download Yungly. Takes under a minute.

2

Link your account

Connect your bank account to send money. Fully secure, RBI-compliant.

3

Set limits & send

Choose spending limits and send the first pocket money. Done!

4

Stay in the loop

Get real-time alerts and monthly summaries. Always informed.
