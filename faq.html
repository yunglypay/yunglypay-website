Everything you need to know about Yungly.

Getting Started

Yungly is a teen-focused banking app built for Indian teenagers aged 13–18. It gives teens their own account and card, while giving parents full visibility and control over spending.

Yungly is available for teens aged 13 to 18. A parent or guardian needs to be involved in the sign-up process since it's a minor's account.

Under 5 minutes. Download the app, enter your details, have your parent approve — and you're live. Your physical card ships within 3–5 business days.

No, you don't need your own bank account. Yungly provides you with a full account. Your parents just need a bank account to send you money.

Fees & Charges

Yes — Yungly is completely free for teens. No monthly fees, no transaction fees, no hidden charges. Ever.

Nope. Parents can send money, set limits, and monitor spending — all for free.

We earn a small interchange fee each time you use your card — paid by the merchant, not you. This keeps Yungly free for both teens and parents.

Card & Payments

Everywhere — Zomato, Swiggy, Steam, Amazon, D-Mart, and any store or website that accepts card payments. Online and offline.

Yes! Your Yungly account works with GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and all major UPI apps.

Freeze your card instantly from the app with one tap. Once you find it (or get a new one), unfreeze or order a replacement — all from the app.

Safety & Security

Yes. Yungly operates in partnership with licensed banking/PPI partners (subject to regulatory approval). Your money is protected under standard banking security protocols.

Yes — parents can see all transactions in real time. This is by design, and it's what makes Yungly safe and trustworthy for families.

We collect only what's needed to operate your account — name, phone, transaction data. We never sell your data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
